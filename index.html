About Us

With a view to bring into existence a centrally controlled administered mechanism involving Pensioners Associations in the country,
the Pensioners' Portal would function as a single window mechanism
The department of Pension Pensioners' Welfare is the nodal department for formulation of policies relating to pension &
other retirement benefits of Central Government Pensioners/Family Pensioners.
It also serves as a forum for redress of Pensioners' Grievances
